Privacy Policy for Push Box

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Introduction

Push Box ("App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Game Data

  • Level completion status and progress
  • Solution playback credits earned
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Move counts and statistics

1.2 App Settings

  • User preferences (sound settings, control options)
  • These are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences

1.3 Advertising Data

  • We use Google AdMob to display advertisements
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and other data to personalize ads
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving the App
  • Tracking your game progress locally
  • Displaying relevant advertisements
  • Debugging and troubleshooting issues
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All game data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences
  • No personal information is collected or stored on external servers
  • Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored data

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

  • Service: Mobile advertising
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Data: Device identifiers, IP address, advertising identifiers

Google Mobile Ads SDK

  • Purpose: To serve ads within the App
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Opt-out: You can disable personalized ads in your device settings
  • Data Deletion: Uninstall the app to delete all local game data
  • Sound: You can toggle sound on/off in the App settings

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App stores only game progress locally on the device.

7. Data Retention

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device indefinitely
  • Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all local data
  • No data is stored on external servers

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App or by other means required by law.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: creationsaam@gmail.com

10. Compliance

  • GDPR: The App complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU)
  • CCPA: The App complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act
  • COPPA: The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

By using Push Box, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
